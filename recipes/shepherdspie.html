Shepherd's Pie

This recipe is a fast and easy way to make a shepherds pie with a delicious cheesy crust.

Ingredients

  • 400g of sweet potatoes
  • 400g of standard potatoes
  • 100g of carrots
  • 2 onions
  • Gravy granules (Bisto)
  • 200g of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Fry mince until brown, dicing potatoes and adding halfway.
  2. Mash and boil the potatoes, adding salt and pepper (1 pinch of each).
  3. Dice carrots and and make gravy, then add to the mince at a simmer.
  4. Add mince and other ingredients to a large baking pot, then the potatoes on top.
  5. Add the grated cheese to the top of the pie.
  6. Place in the oven for 25 minutes at 200C.
